Why Things! emerged?

Due to the groing concern and injustice surrunding dominant digital-retail platforms, that have a predatory and destructive behavors.

An in that sense, its inteded to be the first alternative in this digital-retail secetor, in between a growing ecosystem of many different alternative sectors that are emerging

The mission

  1. Contributing to a more equal and sustanabile future
  2. Making a stronger and wider alternative economy ecosystem
  3. Empowering communities with fincial resources, tools and voice
  4. Sell afforable and sustanabile products

Why in the United States?

Alternative economy not very developed + a growing social intrest in cooperatives and not-for-profit companies

Growing progressive cultural change + tehcnological capacities + innovation culture

The governance system

Any person can join to be a member, and elect and be elected for the company's board

The organizational structure will have a top-level entity, intermediate entities, and an independent entities

The system will rely in diferent types participatory mechanisms: indrect-representative, direct-unmediated, and fluid-mix

Global State

Set data-state="something" on a slide and "something" will be added as a class to the document element when the slide is open. This lets you apply broader style changes, like switching the page background.
